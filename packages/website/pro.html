How do annual and lifetime prices compare?

Annual is a discount on monthly. Lifetime is a one-time payment that never expires — pay once, own PRO for as long as Web Maker exists. Exact prices are shown at checkout (LemonSqueezy).

I use the Chrome extension. How do I upgrade?

Checkout happens on the web app. From the extension, the Get PRO button opens the web app — log in, complete checkout, then come back to the extension and refresh. Your PRO status syncs to the same account in both places.

What payment methods are supported?

Payments are processed by LemonSqueezy and accept all major credit cards, plus PayPal, Apple Pay and Google Pay where available. VAT and local taxes are handled automatically.

Can I cancel anytime? What about refunds?

Monthly and annual subscriptions cancel anytime — no lock-in. If you're within 30 days of purchase and PRO didn't work out, email for a full refund, no questions asked.
